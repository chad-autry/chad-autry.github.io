  • hex-widget
    An HTML5 paper.js based widget for drawing hexagonal grids with items. I've developed it primarily with my HexGalactic project in mind, but it should be easily adaptable to other hex grid turn based games.
    Features:
    • Customizeable gridlines
    • Skewed perspective (nearly isometric) to show multiple items per hex cell
    • Scrollable "windowed" view for when there are too many items per cell for even the skewed perspective
    • Overrideable datasource to support filters/paging/grouping/sorting
    • Overrideable drawn item factory allows customization of art style with SVG support from paper.js
    • Backdrop layer. Used for a star field in HexGalactic, could be made to support geography, clouds, or water for land, air, or sea games.
    • Foreground layer for vectors, badges, borders, and other indicators
    • Mouse controls for item selection
